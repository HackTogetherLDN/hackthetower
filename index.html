Hack the Tower - London hackday

A hack day for developers to work on projects together, build new apps or just discover how to get the most out of all the new shiny technology out there. There are no rules or prizes to distract you, except the infinitely valuable prize of knowledge, experience and fun.

At the start of the hackday we share any project ideas we have, or technology we want to discover. This allows everyone to get into groups and work together to create apps and learn new stuff.

Rough agenda

The structure of the day is pretty open and follows this rough schedule:

  • 09.30 - 10.30 Arrive at the Salesforce office in Heron Tower
  • 10.30 - Discuss your project ideas or technology you want to try out.
  • 10.45 - Split into groups and get going
  • 13.00 - Lunch time (sandwiches, salads, fruit and drinks provided)
  • 13.30 - Keep on hacking until you have had enough
  • 16.00 - Show off projects (optional) or keep on hacking...

Eventually head to the pub, open air roof top resteraunt or back home to feed the kittens...
